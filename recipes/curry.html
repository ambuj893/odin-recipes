Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Pea
  • Salt
  • Brinjal
  • Spices
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables and wash them
  2. Wash the vegetables
  3. Take Oil in a Cooker and put it on the gas.
  4. Now heat the oil for 2 minutes on slow flame and add spices to it.
  5. Add vegetables,water ans salt and mix them
  6. Close the cooker and wait till 2 whistle blow off and wait for some time
  7. Sprinkle and serve
