Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken Pieces
  • Salt
  • Elaichi
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Water
  • Gravy paste

Steps

  1. Take water in a Cooker and put it on the gas.
  2. Now heat the oil for 2 minutes on slow flame and add gravy paste to it.
  3. Now add rice cook for 2 minutes
  4. Now add chicken pices and salt
  5. Close the cooker and wait till 2 whistle blow off
  6. Serve the biryani
