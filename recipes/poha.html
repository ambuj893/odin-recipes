Poha

Description

Ingredients

  • Chura
  • Pea
  • Salt
  • Namkeen

Steps

  1. Take water in a Cooker and put it on the gas.
  2. Now heat the water for 2 minutes on slow flame and add chura to it.
  3. Add salt and cover the lid and wait for 10 minutes
  4. Open the cooker and garnish with namkeen
  5. Sprinkle and serve
